About PearlSky

Uganda's Gateway to Wild Wonders

PearlSky Limited is a domestic airline offering premium scheduled and charter flights into Uganda's national parks. We operate from Entebbe International Airport, connecting you directly to the country's most iconic wildlife destinations.

Flying with us means skipping long road journeys and arriving refreshed, ready to encounter gorillas, lions, chimpanzees, and the mighty Nile in their natural habitat.

Where We Fly

Uganda's Most Spectacular Destinations

From misty mountain gorilla forests to vast savannah plains — every destination is a once-in-a-lifetime experience.

Kasese & Mweya Airstrips

Queen Elizabeth National Park

Home to tree-climbing lions, elephants, hippos & the famous Kazinga Channel boat cruise.

Kihihi & Kisoro Airstrips

Bwindi Impenetrable Forest

UNESCO World Heritage Site. Home to half the world's remaining mountain gorillas.

Bugungu, Pakuba & Chobe

Murchison Falls National Park

Witness the Nile squeeze through a 7-metre gorge — the world's most powerful waterfall.

Western Uganda

Rwenzori Mountains National Park

The "Mountains of the Moon" — Africa's third highest peak, glaciers & exotic Afro-alpine flora.

Western Uganda

Kibale National Park

The "Primate Capital of the World" with the highest density of primates — including 1,500 chimpanzees.

Kisoro, SW Uganda

Mgahinga Gorilla National Park

Rwanda's three Virunga volcanoes meet Uganda — gorillas, golden monkeys & volcanic peaks.

Mweya Peninsular & Kazinga Channel
